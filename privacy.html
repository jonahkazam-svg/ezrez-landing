Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Ez Rez ("we", "our", "us") operates the Ez Rez mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address, display name, and password when you create an account.
  • Preferences: Dining frequency, cuisine preferences, dietary preferences, party size, and referral source collected during onboarding.
  • Watchlist data: Restaurants you choose to track, including party size and preferred dining times.
  • Feedback: Any messages you submit through the feedback or restaurant recommendation features.
  • Device information: Push notification tokens for delivering reservation alerts.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality (tracking restaurant drops and sending notifications).
  • To personalize your experience based on your stated preferences.
  • To send you timely push notifications about reservation availability.
  • To improve the app based on feedback and usage patterns.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS). We use row-level security to ensure users can only access their own data.

Third-Party Services

We link to third-party reservation platforms (Resy, OpenTable, DoorDash, Tock, SevenRooms) but do not share your personal data with them. When you tap to book, you are redirected to their website or app.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at support@ezrez.app.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@ezrez.app.
